ABOUT ME

I’m Than Trong Hien, but you can call me Kai. I’m a Vietnamese.

I was born on 4/7/1996 in Nha Trang and now living in Saigon.

I’m quite a hot tempered person but I also can be the one that make you laugh at parties. I don’t really get along with my family members despite the fact that we both love each other. I don’t like living a too easy and normal life as a life with unexpected things can train me to become a wiser man.

WHAT I’M DOING

I’m studying Bachelor of Design at RMIT South Saigon and working as a freelance graphic designer. I’ve learned a lot of cool things here in RMIT about 2D, illustrating, filming, etc... but I’m sure I want to be a toy designer in the future.

That’s why I’m trying getting as much experience as I can with toys by designing toys, taking toy photos, collecting toys and selling them also.

MY HOBBIES

I do play a lot of computer games as these games train my mind and bring me a lot of inspiration, like Overwatch. I sketch and draw a lot in my free time, too.

I used to go fishing a lot when I was still in Nha Trang. Guitar and drum are the two instruments that I play when I meet up with my band. I love indie rock and jazz a lot. Quai Vat Ti Hon (Indie rock) and Ray Charles (Jazz) are my favorite artists of all time. I smoke when I’m alone.
