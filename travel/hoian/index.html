What to do

Hoi An, also Faifo, is a Vietnamese ancient town in Quang Nam Province. Its Old Town is recognised as the World Heritage Site by UNESCO. Hoi An is famous not only for the peaceful traditional cultural space with breath-taking tourist attractions but also its plentiful in cuisine. Some of the best dining can be had here, from cheap street eats to fabulous five-star feasts. It is also known for clothing, with many shops catering to a very limited pool of tourists.

Why take us with you

Because we have some travel tips for you.
Stay at least a night in Hoi An.
Take your time to enjoy a walk around the Old Town or rent a bike.
