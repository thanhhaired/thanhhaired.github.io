Black Goose Bistro

Located in the center of Paris, Black Goose Bistro offers unparalleled access to the sights, sounds and attractions of France's most vibrant city.

Since the bistro is located at the 25th floor of the tallest building in its area, each of its rooms features breathtaking views of the entire city.
