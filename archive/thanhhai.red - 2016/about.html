what I do

#designer #illustrator #prop-maker #risk-taker

I worked as a designer, illustrator, prop-maker and in some other fields.

As a freelance graphic designer, I'm specializing in print-design, branding and web-design.

I'm also receiving commission as an illustrator, I do concept art and character design and sometimes, environment artist for 2d-game. Making prop for displaying or cosplaying is another hobby of mine.

about

Phan Thanh Hai, 20. Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

I am, imo, a decent guy with a sense of humour. I was born in a family where both of my parent were doing business and none of my relatives working in art-field but my love for art was there in me since I was just a little boy and it has grown with me through out my entire life. I love creating all sort of stuffs and have a great time doing so.

I try my best to develop my skills everyday and there was never a day in my life that I did not learn something new. Even though I was not a good student with an awesome gradebook in highschool, what I love the most is learning new things and expanding my worldview and knowledge.
