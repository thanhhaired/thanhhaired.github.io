About

REbot was create as a school project for Digital Media Studio 5: Recreating from others' creation. The main purpose of the project was take inspiration from existed works of other artist(s) and twisting those works to create your own.

I really enjoy comic superheroes and mech fight at the same time so I decided to take that inspiration and blend them together to create REbot - a project featuring fighting superheroes-look-a-like robots in the scene of old arcade fighting games.

MAR 24 2017 The Indecisive one

After a while, I reallize I don't really like this style very much. I looked at one of my drawing years ago and I noticed that the style of that old painting and the hulk one that I made the other day was the exact same thing, and no one like your new works look identical to one that you made years ago when you was just a noviced.

I'm not anywhere near a decent illustrator but I'm also far from what I was back then, and as more as I look at it, I hate the style of the Hulk-bot, it was too simple, too sloppy, too cramped and too flat. No way in the world I can keep on drawing this without stuck myself up and block my own way of drawing in the rest of this project so I decided that I would have to scrap it and go for a new style.

I will start sketching somes, more on this on the next post.

MAR 21 2017 The First prototype

Well, this is my first finished one. I decided to draw The Hulk because he is closedly resemble to a human being with some easy traits to exaggerate. The Hulk is a very gigantic figure and also a very clumsy one so I tried to draw him with a very bulky body.

You can see that I give the Hulk-bot fists of two heavy metal balls without any fingers for a clumsy look. The short leg also give a feeling that it's not a very fast robot. I use a very cartoonish look to keep the drawing fresh and light, not being too heavy.

MAR 05 2017 THE CONFUSING BRIEF

At the very beginning, I was very confused with what was tasked for, I usually have a specific brief of what to do for every assignment but for this one, the task was to figuring out somethings your own, which kinda confusing for me.

My first idea to “recreating others’ creation” is to mixing characters of an actor across movie. For example, taking Deadpool and Green Lantern, both played by Ryan Reynold and swap them with each other, bringing Deadpool to the Green Lanterns’ universe and also the other way. But later I scrapped the idea and turn it into a different one.

Since I was small, I was really enjoyed arcade platformer fighting games like Street Fighter, Bloody Roar and more recently, Super Smash Bros. I also loves superheroes and robot, so I figure I’m gonna merge theme all together. That was how I came up with the idea to turn supeheroes into robot and put them into to fighting stages just like those 80s’ arcade fighting games.

What’s next was the hard part, give it a name.
